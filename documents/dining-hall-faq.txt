

Frequently Asked Questions
Getting Started
When do meal plans begin and end?
The 2025-26 meal plans run from August 26, 2025 through May 16, 2026.

What are the Dining Hall meal prices?
Breakfast: $9 plus tax
Lunch: $12 plus tax
Dinner: $15 plus tax
Guest payments: Guests paying with Mammoth Bucks or debit/credit card pay the above prices.

How Meal Plans Work
How do I know which dining plan I have?
All first-year students are automatically enrolled in Unlimited. Upperclass students (years 2+) can choose different plans through our selection form. Meal plans can be changed between semesters. However, once plans are activated for the semester, they can no longer be changed.

How do I use my dining plan?
Bring your Amherst ID card to all dining locations. Your card provides access to all-you-care-to-eat meals at the Dining Hall and allows you to use meal swipes at Keefe Grab and Go.

Important: Do not give your ID card to anyone else. Cards are non-transferable. We will confiscate IDs found being used by someone else. If your card is lost or stolen, report it immediately to card services.

New for Fall 2025: Use the Mammoth Mobile App to scan your phone if you forget your card.

What does it mean to have the Unlimited Access plan?
With Unlimited, you can dine at the Dining Hall from morning until night. No counting, no tracking. If you use a meal swipe at Keefe Grab and Go during lunch, you'll need to wait 30 minutes before re-entering the Dining Hall.

How do I check my balances?
Download the Mammoth Mobile App to view your meal plan and balances:

Apple: App Store
Android: Google Play
Using Your Swipes
How do meal swipes work?
Meal swipes are included in your meal plan at no extra cost. Use them at the Dining Hall during any operating hours (breakfast, lunch, dinner, late night) or at Keefe Grab and Go during lunch.

Details:
Available at Keefe Monday through Friday, 11am–2:30pm (excluding holidays)
One swipe per day at Keefe
Weekly swipes reset every Sunday
Semester swipes (Connect Plan) do not reset weekly
How do I use my swipe at Keefe Grab and Go?
Keefe Grab and Go is available Monday through Friday, 11am–2:30pm. Each swipe includes an entrée, 2 sides, and a fountain drink. Using a swipe at Keefe limits access to the Dining Hall until 30 minutes have passed.

Can I use my swipe for takeout from the Dining Hall?
No. Swipes at the Dining Hall are for all-you-care-to-eat dining in the hall. Takeout is not available at the Dining Hall. For grab-and-go meals, visit Keefe Grab and Go during lunch hours.

Mammoth Bucks
Where can I use Mammoth Bucks?
Mammoth Bucks are accepted at these locations:

Frost Library Café
Science Center Café
Mammoth Market (food only)
Vending machines
The Dining Hall
Visit our dining hours page for location information.

What happens if I run out of Mammoth Bucks?
Additional AC Dollars can be added to your meal plan using the Mammoth Mobile App.

What happens if I run out of meal swipes?
Meal plan holders can dine at the Dining Hall at a reduced Mammoth Bucks door rate. Use the full-service line and tell the cashier you're paying with Mammoth Bucks for the reduced rate.

What’s the difference between Mammoth Bucks and AC Dollars?
Mammoth Bucks:
Included with your meal plan
Food only
Works at dining retail locations
Rolls over fall to spring
AC Dollars:
Optional money you add
Food and non-food items
Works everywhere on campus
Never expires while enrolled
Learn more: Mammoth Bucks vs AC Dollars

Guest Meals
Can I bring a guest?
Yes! Students on meal plans receive 8 free guest passes per semester. You can also purchase discounted guest meals with Mammoth Bucks, or guests can pay full door rates.

Do unused guest passes roll over?
No. Unused guest passes do not carry over from semester to semester.

Special Dietary Needs and Services
What if I have special dietary needs or food allergies?
We offer daily allergen friendly meals from our Without 9 station as well as allergen friendly products in a dedicated space in the dining hall.

Contact our nutritionist, Megan Scutti, to discuss your needs.

What is your policy regarding Kosher meals?
We provide Kosher meal service during Passover. Arrangements and sign-ups are coordinated through the Office of Religious and Spiritual Life.

Where can I order a special occasion cake?

Policies
Am I allowed to take any food out of the Dining Hall?
Items allowed to be taken from the Dining Hall
Any beverage (bring your own container; the Dining Hall will not provide to-go cups for Fall 2025)
One piece of fruit, ice cream cone, or dessert that can be carried in your hand
Items not allowed
Dining Hall utensils, china, plastic dishes, salt and pepper shakers, or trays may not be taken from the dining area.

Violation
Students not adhering to these policies must surrender their ID and may pay a $25 fine.

Can someone else use my meal card?
No. Under plan rules, the card is non-transferable. If you're caught using someone else's card or lending your card to someone else, meal checkers will confiscate the card and each party may be fined $25.

Can I change my meal plan during the semester?
No. Meal plans can only be changed at the beginning of each semester. All eligible students receive information via email about meal plan options and the selection period.

Sustainability
Do we recycle waste at the Dining Hall?
We recycle glass, aluminum, tin, plastic, paper, cardboard, and compost food waste from our dining rooms and kitchens.

We're active recyclers using a variety of recycled products in our facilities. Recycled paper is used in copiers, fax machines, and printers. Brochures and publications are printed on recycled paper whenever possible. Unbleached napkins are used in all dining facilities.

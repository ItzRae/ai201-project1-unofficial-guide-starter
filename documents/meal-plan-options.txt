Choose Your Meal Plan
Your meal plan shapes how you eat and connect at Amherst. All residential plans cost the same, so choose based on your lifestyle

First-year students: You're automatically enrolled in Unlimited for your first year.
Upperclass students (Years 2-4+): Choose the plan that fits how you actually eat.
Off-campus students: Stay connected with Connect or choose any residential plan.
Every Plan Includes
Access to the Dining Hall's all-you-care-to-eat dining
Mammoth Bucks for retail and vending locations
Keefe Grab & Go Lunch access
8 guest passes per semester
